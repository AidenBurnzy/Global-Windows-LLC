What We Do

Global Windows LLC is a premier window installation company serving Grand Rapids, Michigan and the surrounding region. We specialize in both residential and commercial window installation, replacement, and repair services.

Global partners with companies to provide the best window installation experience for our customers

With over 40 years of combined experience in the industry, our skilled team delivers professional installation services using only the highest quality materials and proven installation techniques. From single-family homes to large commercial buildings, we handle projects of all sizes with the same commitment to excellence.

Our services include energy-efficient window replacements, new construction installations, commercial glazing, and custom window solutions. Every project is backed by our commitment to customer satisfaction.
